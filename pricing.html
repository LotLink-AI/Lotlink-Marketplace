The Math

A full-time BDC rep loaded with payroll tax, benefits, and management overhead costs $3,000–$5,000/month — and they only work 40 hours, get sick, take PTO, and miss every after-hours lead. Jordan answers in under a second, follows up at 2 AM, never quits, and costs less than half a human's loaded rate even at Pro tier. Roughly 20–30% of dealership leads arrive outside open hours; the rest stack up during lunch, sales meetings, and busy showroom moments. Most dealerships land on Growth because it's the sweet spot for a typical 40–150 car independent. Heavier-volume operators and multi-rooftop dealers should look at Pro — and lock the Founding Partner rate before the 10 spots close. Prepay annually and get two months free (17% off) on Growth or Pro.
