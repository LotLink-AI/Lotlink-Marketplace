What Jordan Does
An agent that works with your team.

Three channels of customer engagement, running 24 hours a day alongside the people you already have. Configure once, ship it to your floor.

📞

24/7 voice answering

Inbound and outbound calls handled by a natural-sounding AI voice. Books appointments, qualifies leads, transfers warm to your sales floor when it's time.

💬

SMS that actually replies

Text follow-up that works the lead — not a one-shot blast. Federal TCPA quiet-hours built in. Two-way conversations Jordan picks up where they left off.

✉️

Personalized emails with vehicle photos

Every email includes the customer's vehicle of interest, a direct link to the VDP, and photos pulled live from your inventory. Not a template — a real message.

🎯

Inventory-aware

Jordan only pitches vehicles you actually have on the lot. Three-layer inventory honesty guard means he never claims stock you don't have. Built after we made that mistake ourselves.

📊

Lead scoring + workplan

Every contact scored 0-100 on engagement, intent, and recency. Your floor walks in to a prioritized "today's workplan" — call these three first, here's why.

🔄

Warm transfer to your team

When a lead is ready to talk to a human, Jordan can warm-transfer to specific reps based on shift, specialty, or who's available. No cold handoffs.

Plus: cadence editor with 4 preset tiers · multi-buyer ADF parsing · automatic voicemail detection · campaign-based outbound · CAN-SPAM compliant unsubscribe · free LotLink marketplace listing included.
