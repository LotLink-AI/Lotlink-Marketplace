The Basics

What LotLink actually is.

  • We're a Northern-Indiana-only vehicle marketplace plus an AI BDC — business development center. Think of us as two things in one: a regional shopping site where buyers across the area find your inventory, and an always-on sales assistant named Jordan that responds to every lead within a minute, qualifies them, and books appointments on your lot. We don't own inventory. We don't compete with you. We make the phone ring.
  • Those are national lead platforms. You pay a monthly fee to appear in a search result alongside hundreds of out-of-state dealers, and when a lead comes in it sits in your inbox while you're with another customer. LotLink is regional (buyers here, not in Nevada) and active (Jordan works every lead the moment it arrives, instead of waiting for you). You can keep your existing platforms — nothing about LotLink requires you to drop them.
  • Your CRM is where leads sit and wait for a human to work them. Jordan is the human — except Jordan doesn't sleep, take lunch, or miss a 7pm lead. LotLink plugs into your existing CRM instead of replacing it: leads still get forwarded through your normal workflow, and Jordan's replies and call transcripts flow back so the CRM stays in sync. Nobody at your dealership changes how they work.
  • LotLink is built and operated by John Roberts out of Rochester, Indiana. John ran a dealership-side BDC for years before building this, and every dealer on the platform has his cell number. You're not buying from a call center in Austin — you're buying from a neighbor who knows what it's like to chase a ghost lead at 9pm.

About Jordan (the AI)

What the AI actually does and doesn't do.

  • Jordan is real AI — natural-language voice, SMS, and email — running on the same kind of models that power ChatGPT and Claude. There's no call center behind it, which is why it can respond in under a minute 24/7 without the overhead of a human BDC. For the edge cases where a lead needs a human (complex trade-in questions, negotiation), Jordan escalates to you directly.
  • Jordan is upfront about being an AI assistant if asked — it won't lie about it — but it sounds natural and courteous, not robotic. The transcripts we publish with every customer show why this works: people ask questions, Jordan answers, people book appointments. Nobody hangs up because "an AI picked up." What they actually hate is leaving a voicemail that never gets returned, which is what happens when they call your lot after 6pm.
  • Jordan escalates. If a customer asks something Jordan isn't sure about — trade-in valuation, specific finance terms, a part replacement question — Jordan takes the customer's info, tells them a human will follow up, and pushes the request to you inside the portal with full context. You never lose a lead to "I don't know."
  • We pull your inventory from your dealership website every hour. When you list a new Silverado or sell your last Civic, Jordan knows about it within the next sync. Jordan can answer specific questions about price, miles, trim level, photos — whatever's on your vehicle detail pages. No manual CRM updates required.
  • Yes. Jordan sets the appointment during the call or text thread, writes it into your LotLink portal, and — if you've connected Google Calendar or Outlook — drops it directly on your team's calendar with the customer's name, phone, and the vehicle they're interested in. You show up, they show up, you sell a car.
  • First outbound SMS goes out within one minute of the lead hitting our intake. Industry research says dealers who respond in five minutes close deals at about three times the rate of those who respond in an hour. Jordan beats five minutes on every lead, every time, without fail.

Leads & Compliance

The legal stuff, answered plainly.

  • Every SMS Jordan sends includes the required HELP/STOP footer and goes out from a TCR-registered A2P 10DLC short code tied to your dealership. We track opt-in source, opt-out events, and consent timestamps for every message — fully auditable if you ever get a compliance complaint. The rules are the same whether a human or an AI sends the text; we just don't forget to follow them.
  • They get an immediate confirmation and are unenrolled from every active campaign in the system — past, current, and future. Jordan will never text or call that number again, period. No human override, no "oh they probably didn't mean it." Opt-out is automatic and absolute.
  • Two ways. First, Jordan recognizes obvious junk patterns (bot emails, solicitors pitching you SEO services) and never engages. Second — and this is the safety net — when you run a campaign enrollment from the portal, every lead appears as a checkbox. You uncheck spam, junk, or test numbers before clicking Enroll. Your campaigns stay clean without any extra work.
  • Never. Each dealership's data is walled off — a salesperson at one dealer cannot see a single lead from another. We don't run a lead-resale operation. The only leads you see are your own, and the only leads we route to you are your own.
