# LotLink — LinkedIn DM Kit

A 5-touch LinkedIn sequence for cold outreach to independent dealer
owners/GMs. Designed for the way LinkedIn actually works:

- Mobile-first (80%+ of LinkedIn is read on a phone)
- Plain text only — no HTML, no bold, no markdown
- No external links in the OPENER (LinkedIn algorithm deprioritizes
  messages with links until a real conversation starts)
- Short. Under 500 chars where possible. Skim-readable.

Search-and-replace tokens: `[FIRST_NAME]`, `[DEALERSHIP]`, `[CITY_OR_REGION]`,
`[SHARED_DETAIL]` (one specific thing from their profile/lot).

----------------------------------------------------------------------

## 01 · Connection Request Note  (200-char limit on free LinkedIn)

```
[FIRST_NAME] — fellow car guy. I run sales at House of Carz in
Rochester, IN. Built an AI BDC at our lot — would love to swap
notes with another indie. No pitch. Promise.
```

Char count: 195 ✓ (fits Basic LinkedIn).
If you have LinkedIn Premium (300 char limit on connect notes):

```
[FIRST_NAME] — fellow indie car guy here. I run sales at House of
Carz in Rochester, IN, and built an AI BDC at our lot that's
been booking appointments at 9pm on Saturdays. Would love to
swap notes with another indie. No pitch, just curious how
you're handling after-hours leads.
```

----------------------------------------------------------------------

## 02 · First DM (Opener — after they accept)

Send 1–3 days AFTER they accept the connection. Don't send the
same day — feels too eager. NO LINK in this message.

```
Thanks for the add, [FIRST_NAME].

Quick story I think you'll appreciate. April this year my lot
got a lead at 9:14 PM Saturday on a Silverado. The kind that
used to sit until Monday morning, cold.

She got a reply in 38 seconds, booked Monday at 10, and drove
home in the truck that afternoon. No one on my team touched it
until she walked in.

I built the system — couldn't keep watching after-hours leads
die. It's three channels (voice, text, email), works 24/7.

Worth a 2-min look? Won't send you anything till you say yes.
```

Char count: 590. Strong opener with story, ends with permission ask.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

## 03 · Reply DM (After they say "sure / yes / interested")

Now you can send the link. Keep it short — they already said yes.

```
Appreciate it, [FIRST_NAME]. Two ways to look:

→ 90-sec walkthrough: lotlinkin.com
→ 15-min live demo (I'll play 3 real House of Carz calls,
   including the Silverado one): calendly.com/john-lotlinkin/demo

Either way I'm around — (260) 229-9393 is my cell. Text me
anything you're curious about.

P.S. We're opening 10 Founding Partner spots with pricing
locked for life. 9 left as of today. Not pressuring — just so
you know it exists.
```

Char count: 470.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

## 04 · Bump Message (5–7 days after #02, no reply)

```
Hey [FIRST_NAME] — totally get it if the timing's off. Last
thing from me unless you say otherwise.

If after-hours leads at [DEALERSHIP] ever feel like they're
slipping through the cracks, the 15-min demo is here when you
want it: calendly.com/john-lotlinkin/demo

Either way — good luck Q3, brother.
```

Char count: 320.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

## 05 · Voice Note Script  (60 seconds — record in LinkedIn mobile app)

This is the unlock for cold outreach in 2026. Almost nobody uses
voice notes. They stand out, they sound human, and dealers — who
live on the phone — actually listen to them.

How to record: open the chat in the LinkedIn mobile app → tap
the microphone icon → hold to record (up to 60 sec) → release.

Script (rehearse it once, then sound natural — don't read):

```
Hey [FIRST_NAME], John Roberts here, House of Carz in Rochester,
Indiana. Real quick — I won't waste your time.

I built an AI BDC at our store after I got tired of watching
9pm Saturday leads die in the inbox. Three channels — voice,
text, email — answers in under a minute, 24/7. Built it for our
lot, now I'm sharing it with other indies.

If you're curious, just shoot me a thumbs up here and I'll send
over a 90-second walkthrough. No pitch deck, no sales call.

Either way, appreciate the add. Have a good one.
```

~50 seconds spoken at conversational pace. Three rules for
voice notes:
  1. Smile while you talk — it changes your tone
  2. State your name and dealership in the first 5 seconds
  3. End with a single ask ("shoot me a thumbs up")

----------------------------------------------------------------------

## CADENCE (recommended)

  Day 0:   Send connection request (#01)
  Day +2:  Send opener DM (#02)               [if they accepted]
  Day +5:  If silent, leave a voice note (#05)
  Day +8:  Send bump message (#04)
  Day +14: Move on. They're not ignoring — they're busy.

Replied at any stage? Send #03 with the demo link.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

## TWO RULES THAT DOUBLE REPLIES

1. PROFILE FIRST. Spend 30 seconds on their LinkedIn before #02
   and find ONE specific detail to mention — a recent post, a
   anniversary, a vehicle their lot featured. Replace the
   generic story setup with: "Saw your post about [X] —
   reminded me of something at our lot..."

2. DON'T SELL FROM #02. The goal of the opener is a YES to
   "want to see it?", not a closed deal. The pitch goes in #03,
   AFTER they've leaned in.
