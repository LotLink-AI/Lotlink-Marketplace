## How the morning agent uses this file

Every weekday at 6:30 AM the agent reads this file and surfaces a "rolodex nudge" section in the daily briefing. Each entry gets a stoplight:

- 🔴 **URGENT** — more than 10 days since last touch
- 🟡 **WARM** — 5–10 days since last touch
- 🟢 **RECENT** — under 5 days since last touch (no nudge needed)

The agent will draft a 1-2 sentence suggested next-message for each URGENT or WARM contact, based on the `last_topic` field. It does NOT auto-send anything to warm contacts — you text/call them from your iPhone after reading the briefing.
