The one-sentence verdicts

  • Podium — the real threat in our price band. 6,000+ dealerships, used-auto page, OpenAI case study. Beat them on contract terms (their Trustpilot is 1.5★ over billing/cancellation), price transparency, and automotive-only depth.
  • Impel — the enterprise umbrella. CEO publicly says full AI stack runs "$5,000+/mo." Explicitly not for single-rooftop dealers. Their price is our pitch.
  • Conversica — shrinking, no voice, $36K/yr floor. Two layoff rounds, 112 employees, email-first. Easy win in our segment.
  • Numa / Toma / STELLA / Brooke — the service-AI wave. All four are service-department-first with franchise DMS moats. Sales BDC is their admitted newest muscle — it's our whole product.
  • Gubagoo / ActivEngage — human-chat incumbents. No AI voice, no outbound. Different sport; only collide on "do you have website chat?" (we do, Growth tier).
  • Whitespace confirmed: nobody combines AI voice + SMS + email + outbound cadences + used-inventory selling in ONE agent at transparent independent-dealer pricing. That is the LotLink lane.
