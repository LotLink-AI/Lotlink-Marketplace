Don't Take Our Word For It — Meet Jordan Yourself

Every AI company will tell you their thing is amazing. We could do that too. But telling isn't the same as feeling — so we built a page where you stop being the dealer and become the customer.

John Roberts
Sales & F&I Manager, House of Carz · Founder, LotLink

Every AI company will tell you their product is amazing. We could do that too — tell you Jordan answers every lead in seconds, books appointments while you sleep, and never takes a day off.

But telling you isn't the same as showing you. And it's definitely not the same as letting you feel it. So we built something different: a page where you stop being the dealer and become the customer.

The lead you miss tonight buys from whoever answered first

Every independent dealer already knows this in their gut. Not the best price. Not the best salesperson. Whoever answered first. And after hours, on a Sunday, in the middle of a test drive — nobody's answering.

Jordan is. He's the AI BDC we built for independent lots, and he answers every lead in seconds — by text, by phone, by email — around the clock. He qualifies the shopper, answers their questions, and books the appointment. Then he hands your salesperson a confirmed customer instead of a cold callback list.

So we built a page where you become the customer

You could read that last paragraph and nod. Or you could feel it. On the new demo page, you can experience the whole thing in about two minutes:

  • Chat with him like a late-night shopper would.
  • Have him call your phone — a real call, right now.
  • Get a real text with the vehicle and a photo — then text him back and watch him keep the conversation going.
  • See the follow-up email your leads would receive, exactly as it lands in their inbox.

That's not a video. That's not a slide deck. That's your customer's experience — happening to you.

After two minutes on that page, you'll know exactly what your shoppers get… and exactly what's been slipping away every night you weren't there to answer.

Why we put it all on one page

We're proud of what we built — but pride isn't the point. We put every channel in one place — chat, call, text, email — because that's the honest test. If Jordan holds up while you're poking at him like a skeptical buyer, he'll hold up for your customers when a real deal is on the line. So go ahead and try to trip him up. That's what the page is for.

Independents have spent years being sold tools built for franchise stores with a five-person BDC. This one was built on a real lot, for lots like yours, by someone who still sells cars every day. The best way to know if it's for you isn't to read another pitch — it's to spend two minutes as your own customer and decide for yourself.

Go Be Your Own Customer
