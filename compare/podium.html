The Honest Fit

We'd rather you pick the right tool than win a deal we shouldn't. Here's our actual read.

Pick Podium if…

  • You run multiple locations across mixed industries and want reviews, payments, and messaging in one corporate platform.
  • Your group already lives in Podium for reputation management and you mainly need to bolt AI onto it.
  • A 12-month commitment doesn't bother you and you have someone to negotiate the quote.

Pick LotLink if…

  • You're an independent used-car dealer whose leads go cold at night and on Sundays because nobody answers.
  • You want one AI agent that answers calls, makes calls, and texts and emails leads for days — not a platform with modules to assemble.
  • You want to know the price before the demo, and the option to walk away monthly if it doesn't earn its keep.

Questions Dealers Ask

How much does Podium actually cost for a dealership?

They don't say publicly — that's the point. 2026 third-party teardowns put base plans at $399–$999+/month with the Jerry AI agent running $99–$399/month extra, plus per-user, per-phone-line, and per-location fees. Realistic all-in estimates with AI land around $700–$1,200/month on a 12-month contract. We publish ours: $899, $1,499, or $2,499 — month-to-month.

Isn't Podium bigger and safer?

Bigger, yes — 6,000+ dealerships and many other industries. Whether that's safer depends on what you need. A platform serving HVAC companies and med spas optimizes for breadth. LotLink does one thing: work car leads for independent dealers, across every channel, around the clock. You also get the founder's cell phone, not a support queue.

What if LotLink doesn't work for my store?

Then you stop paying. Month-to-month, 30-day money-back guarantee, no early-termination math. We win by being worth it every month, not by making it hard to leave.

Can I see it work before I buy?

Yes — book a 15-minute demo and call our AI live during it. Bring your toughest lead scenario.
