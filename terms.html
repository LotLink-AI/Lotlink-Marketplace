13. SMS Messaging Terms (For Consumers)

When a consumer submits a lead, contact form, or test-drive request to a dealership that uses LotLink — or otherwise initiates contact with such a dealership — that consumer may receive recurring text messages from the dealership (or from LotLink on the dealership's behalf) regarding the inquiry.

Program Name

LotLink Dealer Lead Response & Appointment Reminders (the "Program"), operated by LotLink LLC on behalf of subscribing dealerships.

Description

The Program sends one-to-one conversational text messages to acknowledge the consumer's inquiry, ask follow-up questions about the vehicle they're interested in, schedule a test drive or showroom visit, and send appointment reminders. Messages are sent only after the consumer initiates contact with a subscribing dealership.

Message Frequency

Recurring messages tied to your inquiry. A typical conversation runs 2–6 messages over 7 days. Frequency varies based on your replies and the dealer's follow-up cadence.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message at any time to opt out. After opting out you will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts. To re-enroll, contact the dealer directly or text START to the dealer's number.
