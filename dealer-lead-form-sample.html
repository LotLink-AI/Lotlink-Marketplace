Sample dealership vehicle inquiry form

This page is a representative copy of the vehicle-inquiry lead form that a participating dealership displays on its own website and third-party vehicle listings. It is published here so the SMS consent language can be reviewed exactly as a consumer sees it at the point of entry. The form below is for demonstration only and does not submit data.

What this demonstrates: When a consumer inquires about a vehicle at a participating dealership, the standardized SMS consent disclosure shown below appears directly on the inquiry form. The consent checkbox is unchecked by default and optional — the consumer can submit the inquiry without opting in to text messages. No SMS is ever sent unless the consumer actively checks the box.
