## Summary

## Key Platforms Compared

**Podium**
- A mid-sized dealer closed 68% more sales post-adoption, with AI follow-ups generating 148 additional units annually.
- Pricing starts at approximately $399/month as an add-on to core Podium plans.
- Scales to $2,000+/month for dealer groups.
- Transforms every chat, text, and review reply into a booking opportunity.
- Schedules test drives, answers inventory questions, recovers ghosted leads.
- Integrates with VinSolutions, Tekion, and more.

**Numa**
- Service profits rose 40% for adopters.
- AI handles missed calls to recover $853,000 in potential revenue (case study).
- Enterprise-grade communication layer that wraps the entire BDC.
- Voice agents handle every call 24/7.
- Real-time sentiment analysis detects buying signals or frustration.
- Multichannel hub unifying phone, text, email, and chat into one dashboard.

**Toma AI**
- Specializes in inbound automation.
- High appointment booking rates.
- Booked 9,000+ appointments in 90 days for one group (unlocking $2M in revenue).

**STELLA Automotive AI**
- Voice-focused for "always-on" operations.
- Ideal for service departments.
- Positions itself as hybrid workforce: STELLA handles repetitive/low-level tasks and unqualified sales leads, filtering high-intent opportunities to human closers.

## Integration Landscape

Most platforms (Podium, Numa) offer APIs for Elead, VinSolutions, and other CRMs, syncing leads in real-time.
STELLA, Numa, and Virtual BDC sync with CDK, Reynolds, and Tekion.
