# The 2026 Modern Dealership Communication Stack

## Summary

Matador AI presents its vision for what a modern dealership communication stack should include in 2026 — a unified, AI-powered omnichannel platform.

## Core Components of the Modern Stack

Matador's answer:
- Calls, texts, emails, social messages, and website chats connected into one seamless conversation handled instantly by AI.
- Calls, chat, SMS, video, and CRM data working together through a single unified platform.
- Full visibility into every interaction across every channel.
- AI-driven appointment confirmation, reminders, and follow-up to boost show rate.

## Key Features

- **Unified Inbox** — All customer messages from every channel in one dashboard.
- **Video Integration** — Walkaround videos and video messaging.
- **Omnichannel Engagement** — Full multi-channel coverage.
- **CRM Integration** — Deep data sync with existing dealership CRMs.
- **Automation** — AI manages appointment lifecycle end-to-end.
- **Smart Prioritization** — Matador highlights the most important opportunities and tells teams where to act next.

## Pricing (from third-party comparisons)

- **Core tier:** $300/month (up to 150 vehicles, 2,500 messages)
- **Plus tier:** $500/month (up to 400 vehicles, 8,000 messages)
- **Pro tier:** $1,000/month (up to 1,000 vehicles, 20,000 messages)

Note: Matador is primarily built for franchise dealer groups, so enterprise pricing may differ.

## Features That Stand Out

- Social message integration (Facebook DM, Instagram DM) is less common in competitor products.
- Video walkarounds integrated into conversation flow.
- Automatic appointment confirm/remind/follow-up sequences.

## Takeaway for LotLink

Matador's unified inbox is a feature worth mimicking. Adding web chat + social DM ingestion would close a feature gap. Their pricing tier model (by vehicle count / message volume) is a workable template for LotLink's own tiering — aligns product consumption with dealer size.
