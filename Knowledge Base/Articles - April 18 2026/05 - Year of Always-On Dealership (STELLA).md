## Summary

STELLA's thought-leadership piece on why 2026 is the inflection year for always-on dealerships powered by voice AI. Article argues that voice AI has matured to the point where dealers can operate 24/7 without adding headcount.

## Core Thesis

An "always-on dealership" means:
- Every call answered, 24/7/365.
- No missed leads during after-hours, lunch breaks, or high-volume periods.
- Seamless channel switching (voice → SMS → web chat) mid-conversation.
- Proactive outbound campaigns alongside inbound coverage.
