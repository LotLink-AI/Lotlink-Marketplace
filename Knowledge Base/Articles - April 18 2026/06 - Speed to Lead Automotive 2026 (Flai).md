## Summary

Comprehensive guide to speed-to-lead strategy for dealerships in 2026. The central thesis: the first dealership to respond wins, and AI is the only way to guarantee sub-minute response times at scale.

## Critical Statistics

### Response Time Impact
- Dealerships that respond within **15 minutes close 50% more leads**.
- Responding in **under 5 minutes improves closing ratios by up to 900%**.
- **78% of customers buy from the dealership that responds first.**
- Responding within 5 minutes makes you **7x more likely to connect meaningfully**.
- Top-performing BDCs convert **80% of inbound calls** vs. the 50% industry standard.

### After-Hours Lead Volume
- **56-60% of leads arrive after business hours.**
- Only **37% of leads get addressed within the first hour** (McKinsey, January 2025).

### Missed Call Impact
- Missed calls cost the average dealership **over $1M yearly**.
- **31.8% of customers hang up** while on hold.
- **32.3% leave voicemails** that often go unreturned.
- **70% of people who hit voicemail call a competitor within 30 minutes.**

## Recommended Response Targets

- **Live phone calls:** Under 30 seconds (industry leader target).
- **Digital leads:** Under 5 minutes.
- **SMS:** Under 1 minute.
- **Form submissions:** Under 5 minutes.

## Recovery Tactics

- Live recovery text within seconds of a missed call.
- Recovers a significant portion of leads that would otherwise go to competitors.
- NPS frequently rises 4+ points from the proactive touch.

## Why AI Wins This Metric

- Humans cannot staff 24/7 with under-5-minute response at volume.
- AI responds in seconds regardless of time of day.
- Cost per lead responded to is a fraction of human staffing.
- Top-performing BDCs are increasingly AI-augmented.
