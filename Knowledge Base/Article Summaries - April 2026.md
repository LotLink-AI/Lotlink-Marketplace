## 1. Running a BDC in an AI World: A Dealer's Playbook for 2026
**Source:** BDC.AI — https://www.bdc.ai/research/running-a-bdc-in-an-ai-world

This playbook argues that AI in early 2026 has reached a point where it can detect tone, adjust responses, handle interruptions naturally, and speak 10+ languages fluently. For high-volume qualification calls, most customers cannot tell the difference between AI and well-trained human reps.

The best-performing dealerships run a hybrid model: AI handles volume (first touch, qualification, scheduling, follow-up), while humans handle nuance — trade negotiation, finance conversations, and objections requiring empathy and experience.

A key insight is that an AI-powered BDC is becoming the most valuable data asset in a dealership. While a CRM stores records, an AI-powered BDC creates intelligence by listening, parsing, and understanding what customers actually want.

## 2. AI BDC: How AI Will Transform Automotive Dealership BDCs in 2026
**Source:** Traver Connect — https://traverconnect.com/blog/ai-bdc

Traver Connect positions the AI BDC as the next evolution of the traditional Business Development Center — AI as a powerful partner to human agents, not a replacement. Key transformations include:

- **Intelligent Lead Scoring:** AI analyzes thousands of data points from a lead's digital behavior, going far beyond traditional simple filters.
- **Smart Call Routing:** AI analyzes initial caller intent and instantly routes them to the most appropriate BDC rep (sales inquiry vs. service appointment).
- **Automated Re-engagement:** Hyper-personalized text and email sequences trigger based on specific customer actions or inactivity, bringing dormant leads back into the funnel while humans focus on active conversations.
- **Efficiency Gains:** AI handles sorting, initial lead response, and re-engagement, allowing existing teams to accomplish more without increasing headcount.

The overall customer experience improves because customers reach the right expert faster and agents are better prepared for each conversation.

## 3. Lotlinx Uses AI & Analytics to Move Cars Faster
**Source:** CBT News (Kerri Wise interview) — https://www.cbtnews.com/lotlinx-uses-ai-analytics-to-move-cars-faster/

Kerri Wise, CMO at Lotlinx, outlined two major product launches at NADA 2026:

- **LotGPT:** A dealer-focused chatbot providing real-time guidance on inventory decisions. It is the industry's first dealer-facing AI advisor and is offered free to dealerships.
- **Revive:** Targets previously closed-loss leads through automated outreach. Pilot data shows approximately 20% of closed-loss leads return to dealership websites within 45 days, representing incremental sales at lower acquisition cost.

The platform analyzes millions of VINs and shopper interactions daily, using predictive models to identify cars that may struggle to sell and creating targeted campaigns to connect those vehicles with active in-market buyers. Dealerships using Lotlinx see cars sell 37% faster than local market averages, with reduced markdowns and lower holding costs.

Wise also noted that generic AI tools continue to fall short for dealers — automotive-specific AI is essential.

## 5. Inside Van Horn's AI-Powered BDC Playbook
**Source:** DealershipGuy — https://news.dealershipguy.com/p/inside-van-horn-s-ai-powered-bdc-playbook-that-s-rewriting-dealership-follow-up-2025-11-04

Van Horn Automotive's Sales BDC Manager Brittney Dean has built a model where AI amplifies human connection rather than replacing it. Key components:

- **Sub-Minute Response Times:** Lead response on chat/text is roughly 30 seconds, day or night. AI handles instant inbound replies so humans can focus on outbound relationship-building.
- **Structured Onboarding:** A four-week program trains every rep on policy, process, phone skills, and AI collaboration.
- **Daily "Save-A-Deal" Meetings:** Keeps accountability high and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.
- **Segmented BDC Operations:** Specialized segments for credit-tier follow-up and post-sale outreach.
- **Proactive Customer Mining:** Reps are required to use every available tool to "mine" for previous customers who haven't visited in over five years.

This is a real-world case study showing the hybrid model in action at a multi-location dealer group.

## 7. The Human Side of AI in Automotive BDC
**Source:** Strolid — https://strolid.com/human-side-ai-automotive/

Strolid's core argument is that AI should support — not replace — BDC agents. A 2024 study found 80% of dealerships across the U.S. and Canada are already using or planning to deploy AI in BDC, but many don't pair AI with human input, which is critical for best results.

Key capabilities of their approach:

- **AI Transcription & Sentiment Analysis:** Logs and tracks all dealership calls, emails, texts, and replies, then analyzes them for sentiment and important data to generate more personalized campaigns and responses.
- **Intelligent Lead Scoring:** AI analyzes customer interactions and historical data to predict which leads are most likely to convert.
- **VCON Technology:** Strolid's proprietary system balances AI with human insights while maintaining federal and state compliance.

The takeaway: AI is a transformative force, but only when it's focused on augmenting people rather than replacing them with machines.
