Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

LotLink Indiana ("LotLink," "we," "us") operates lotlinkin.com and provides auto brokerage and concierge vehicle buying services in Northern Indiana. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Contact details — name, phone number, email address, and physical address when you fill out one of our forms.
  • Vehicle interest — the vehicle you're inquiring about, budget, trade-in info, and your preferences.
  • Financing inquiry information — employment, approximate income range, and desired monthly payment when you request pre-approval assistance. We do not collect Social Security numbers, full date of birth, or bank account numbers through this website. A formal credit application, if you choose to proceed, is completed through a secure lender portal operated by our financing partners.
  • Dealer partner information — if you represent a dealership, the business name, your role, and inventory details.

Information collected automatically

  • Standard web logs (IP address, browser type, pages viewed, referring site) via our hosting provider.
  • Basic analytics data to understand how the site is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your vehicle or financing inquiry.
  • Search partner dealerships for vehicles matching your criteria.
  • Introduce you to an appropriate lender or dealer partner to complete a purchase.
  • Provide the concierge service you've requested, including paperwork and delivery coordination.
  • Improve the site and our services.
  • Comply with legal obligations that apply to Indiana-licensed auto brokers.

3. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only as needed to fulfill your request:

  • Dealer partners in Northern Indiana, when introducing you to a specific vehicle.
  • Lending partners, when you ask us to help you get pre-approved. You will be directed to the lender's own secure application before any credit check is performed.
  • Service providers that help us operate — form handling (Formspree), hosting (Netlify), email, and analytics.
  • Legal and regulatory requests, when required by law.

We do not sell your personal information.

Mobile information. Mobile phone numbers and any related opt-in or text-message data collected by LotLink Indiana or by participating dealers using the LotLink platform will not be shared, sold, or rented to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Phone numbers may be shared only with the dealer you submitted an inquiry to and with the service providers (such as Twilio) that deliver the messages on the dealer's behalf.

3a. SMS Text Messaging

If you submit a vehicle inquiry, contact form, financing request, or test-drive request through this Site, or you initiate contact directly with a participating dealer, you may receive recurring text messages from the dealer (or from LotLink on the dealer's behalf) regarding your inquiry. Submission of an inquiry constitutes express written consent to receive these messages.

  • Frequency: recurring conversational messages tied to your inquiry — typically 2–6 messages over 7 days, varying with your replies.
  • Message and data rates may apply per your wireless carrier plan.
  • To opt out, reply STOP to any message. You will receive one final confirmation and no further messages.
  • For help, reply HELP, email john@lotlinkin.com, or call 260-229-9393.

Full SMS Terms are published at lotlinkin.com/terms.html.

4. Your Choices

  • Opt out of contact — reply STOP to any text message, or email us to be removed from our lists.
  • Access or delete your data — email john@lotlinkin.com and we will respond within a reasonable time.
  • Cookies — you can configure your browser to block cookies; the site will still function.

5. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect information in our possession. All web forms submit over HTTPS. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. For this reason we deliberately limit the sensitive fields collected through this website and direct formal credit applications to secure lender portals.

6. Children

This site is intended for people 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the most recent changes took effect.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email john@lotlinkin.com or call 260-229-9393.
